The Project

“Composition NHV” is a collaborative Public Humanities project conceived as part of "Introduction to Public Humanities", a Spring 2023 course at Yale University. We have sought to implement a collaborative workflow rooted in consensus-based decision-making on issues that relate to the project as a whole including the project's design, content, and team roles.
